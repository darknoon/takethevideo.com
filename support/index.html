Take Support

FAQ

How do I delete a video?

  1. Open the Photos application from your home screen
  2. Select Camera Roll from the Albums list
  3. Tap a video to view it, then tap the trash icon in the bottom right to delete
  4. Alternately, you can delete multiple videos by selecting Edit in Camera Roll and tapping multiple videos in the list.

(A security measure in iOS prevents Take from directly deleting videos.)

I see a green pointy button and cannot use Take

Sorry about that! This is a bug that sometimes happens when you return to Take after using it the first time. Force-quitting Take will permanently fix this issue:

  • Hit the home button to leave Take
  • Double-tap the home button to bring up the multitasking tray
  • Hold down on the Take icon until the icons dance
  • Tap the red (-) button next to Take
  • Relaunch Take from the home screen

I'm experiencing an error when opening the app

Take requires your permission to save videos. If there is a misconfiguration you might encounter one of the following errors:

  • Photos Access Required (iOS 6)
  • Camera Roll Access Restricted (iOS 6)
  • Location Access Required (denied) (iOS 5)
  • Location Access Required (restricted) (iOS 5)
